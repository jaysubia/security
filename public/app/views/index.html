About Us

Innovative Security is an exclusive private security company that provides protection for numerous entertainment productions.

Film set, special events, concerts, TV shows and commercial experience. Innovative Security is a certified PPO licensed security company that provides uniformed guards and event staff that assist with production’s every need. We come equipped with cones, radios, and metal detectors if necessary. We specialize in crowd management and we are dedicated to maintaining the safety and privacy of the entire cast and crew of every production, as well as ensuring the preservation of all equipment. In addition to that protection, our guards are ready and willing to assist with additional services upon request, ranging from trash pick up to traffic control. At Innovative Security, we pride ourselves on having the most professional and discreet staff in the industry, and we look forward to serving you.
